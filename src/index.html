How periods are decided. Each flight's clock time is matched against the airport's published night and shoulder windows. Wrap-around windows like 23:30–06:00 are handled correctly. A flight exactly on a boundary counts in the period that's starting.

About the data. Flightradar24 movement lists are a best-available public record, not the airport's official log — a few movements can be missing or mislabelled. Treat the counts as well-evidenced indicators, not certified figures. This tool deliberately leaves out aircraft-noise (QC) scoring, which needs verified per-variant values; use your full notebook when you need that depth.
